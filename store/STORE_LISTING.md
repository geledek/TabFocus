# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Extension Name
TabFocus

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Manage browser tabs efficiently. Focus on one tab group at a time to reduce clutter and stay productive.

## Detailed Description
TabFocus helps you take control of browser tab chaos by letting you focus on one tab group at a time.

**Key Features:**

• **View One Group at a Time** - Hide other tab groups to focus on what matters. No more distraction from dozens of open tabs.

• **Smart Tab Search** - Instantly find any tab with fuzzy search. Just start typing and jump to the tab you need.

• **Auto-Group by Domain** - Automatically organize tabs by website. Keep related tabs together effortlessly.

• **Find Duplicates** - Detect and close duplicate tabs with one click. Free up memory and reduce clutter.

• **Save & Restore Sessions** - Save your current tab setup and restore it later. Perfect for project-based workflows.

• **Tab Suspension** - Automatically suspend inactive tabs to save memory. Tabs restore instantly when clicked.

• **Dark Mode Support** - Seamless integration with your system theme.

• **Keyboard Shortcuts** - Quick access with customizable shortcuts.

**Why TabFocus?**

Most tab managers help you organize tabs. TabFocus goes further by letting you truly focus - showing only the tabs you need right now while keeping others safely hidden.

Perfect for:
- Developers juggling multiple projects
- Researchers managing many sources
- Anyone who opens "just one more tab"

**Privacy First**
TabFocus works entirely locally. No data is sent to external servers. Your browsing stays private.
