# Privacy Policy for TabFocus

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

TabFocus is a browser extension that helps you manage and organize your browser tabs. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**TabFocus does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data processed by TabFocus remains entirely on your local device.

## Data Storage

TabFocus stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API:

- **Tab group information**: Names, colors, and organization of your tab groups
- **Saved sessions**: Tab URLs and titles when you save a session
- **Extension settings**: Your preferences for the extension

This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers.

## Permissions

TabFocus requires the following browser permissions:

- **tabs**: To read and manage your browser tabs
- **tabGroups**: To create and organize tab groups
- **storage**: To save your settings and sessions locally
- **alarms**: To check for idle tabs (for tab suspension feature)
- **contextMenus**: To add right-click menu options

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality.

## Third-Party Services

TabFocus does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
